Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

For general inquiries, we typically respond within 24 hours during business days. For emergency support, our team is available 24/7 and will respond as soon as possible.

Do you offer support outside business hours?

Yes, our emergency support team is available 24/7 for critical issues. Standard support hours are Monday-Friday, 8am-8pm EST.

Can you help with hardware issues remotely?

We can diagnose many hardware issues remotely and provide guidance. For physical repairs, we can recommend local trusted partners or arrange on-site support where available.

What information should I include in my support request?

Please include details about your system, a clear description of the issue, any error messages, and when the problem started. Screenshots are also very helpful.
